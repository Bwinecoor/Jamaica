Airport Info, Taxi, and Weather

Once you finish getting all of your luggage you can start your Journey and enjoy your stay.

To get a shuttle, step outside and look around for one. When you find it, go to the driver and request a ride. You can either get a taxi or a shuttle. Taxis will take just you and your companians and shuttles will take several people and are scheduled.
